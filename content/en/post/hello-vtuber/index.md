## Hi there

**VTuber Idea Notebook** is a small, growing collection of ideas to make
your streams a bit more fun. We aim for **ideas you can try on today's
stream**, not big theory.

## Four opening themes

- **Stream Ideas** — chat prompts, audience-participation, collab premises
- **Gaming Ideas** — title picks, restrictions, framing angles
- **Singing Streams** — set lists, structure, audio and gear
- **Creator Tips** — streaming tech, social ops, thumbnails, scheduling

## Coming up first

1. Seeding small laughs every five minutes in a chat stream
2. A checklist for picking games that retain first-time viewers
3. How to pick the last song of a karaoke stream without agonizing

Stay tuned. Subscribe to the RSS feed or watch the GitHub repo for updates.
