## Yabusameryoma (やぶさめりょうま)

Full-stack engineer by day, hobbyist streamer and creator-tools builder by night. Based in Japan, deeply into VR.

---

## From PlayStation VR to Streaming

It started with a PlayStation VR on launch day in 2016.

Since then: Oculus Go → HTC VIVE Pro → Meta Quest 3 → **PlayStation VR2 + mocopi (PCVR)**, with an **Apple Vision Pro** on the side (purchased for ¥600,000, now primarily a Disney+ device — though I hold out hope for VRChat support).

The VR rabbit hole eventually led to VTuber-style streaming on YouTube and Twitch. My honest description is "a regular person doing something similar for technical curiosity reasons," but "indie VTuber" is close enough.

---

## Why I Made This Blog

Once I started streaming regularly, I found myself constantly thinking about the same kinds of problems: what to stream next, how to structure a setlist, how to make announcements people actually see.

Being an engineer, my instinct is to turn recurring problems into systems. When I noticed streamers manually making weekly schedule images for X, I built the [Weekly Schedule Image Maker](https://weekcal.vvil.jp/) to automate it.

Over time, the things I figured out started to feel useful beyond just me. **Virtual Village** is where I write them down — streaming tips and creator ops advice from an engineer's perspective.
