## What is this?

**VTuber Idea Notebook** is a slowly-growing collection of stream ideas,
show formats, set lists, and behind-the-scenes tips for VTubers and the
people supporting them.

- Who is it for? — upcoming debuts, mid-tier creators looking for a spark, backstage crew
- What will you find? — stream formats, gaming bits, karaoke set ideas, technical and ops tips
- What won't you find? — drama, call-outs, or gossip about specific people

## Editorial stance

- We don't punch down.
- Posts get updated when they go stale.
- Comments are off for now — talk to us on social instead.
